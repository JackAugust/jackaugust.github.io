Cross-cohort Healthcare Data Security Management Platform Demo

Apr 01, 2023 – Apr 02, 2023

Ginza Springs Hotel

Event: Project Workshop

In the seminar of the National Key Research and Development Programme “Research and Development of Flexible Big Data Cloud Platform Support System for Large Inter-cohort Joint Research”, I, on behalf of the three units of Subject 2, will report the research progress of Subject 2 from the beginning of the project to the present day, and demonstrate the cross-cohort medical data security management platform on behalf of the subject group, so as to reflect the completion of Subject 2’s targets on Access control, traceability audit, etc.’. Criticisms and corrections from experts and teachers are welcome.
