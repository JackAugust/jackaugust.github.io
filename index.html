Biography

Jie Zhang (Student Member, IEEE) received the M.S. degree in Computer Application Technology from the Yunnan Normal University in 2022. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology with the Tianjin University. He has published multiple scientific papers in international journals and conference proceedings, such as CSCWD, TASE, JSTARS, Sustainability, etc. He has served as the PC member for important international conferences and a reviewer for prestigious journals, including IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing (TDSC) and International Conference on Data-driven Optimization of Complex Systems (DOCS'2023). His research interests include efficient and secure data sharing within blockchain systems.
