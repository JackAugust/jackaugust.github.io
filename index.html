Information

Jie Zhang (Student Member, IEEE) received the M.S. degree in Computer Application Technology from the Yunnan Normal University in 2022. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology with the Tianjin University. He has served as the organizing committee member for important international conferences and a reviewer for prestigious journals, including IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing (TDSC). His research interests include efficient and secure data sharing within blockchain systems.
